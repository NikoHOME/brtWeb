TechPad International Ltd is a UK based company with international activities, which has been created to create a complex solution for technical support within several industries.
Firefighting – We are proud to have several years of experience within design, installation and servicing of firefighting systems mainly installed within large commercial premises such as power plants, paper manufactures, wood industry production, automotive production, etc.
Steel prefabrication – We have large workshop where we are able to prefabricate any steel construction for ship industry. We have a large CNC plasma (3m x 12m) where we are able to cutout metal sheets up to 60mm thick.
Underwater activities – We are constantly expanding and one of the recent tasks which we are able to undertake is underwater works. We have equipped our fleet with boats and equipment which allows us to carryout difficult underwater activities – welding, cutting, etc. Owning underwater drone allows us to do any underwater inspections of ground, hulls, etc.
Overwater activities – We have a team of specialists who are ready to preform technical support on the water for small or large vessels.
